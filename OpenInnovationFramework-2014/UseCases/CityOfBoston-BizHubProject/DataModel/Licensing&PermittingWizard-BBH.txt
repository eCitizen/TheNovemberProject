Licensing and Permitting Wizard Notes:


Step 1 Initial Questions

This Wizard is meant to help you figure out what Licenses and Permits you may need in order to open or expand your business. Based upon your answers to the following questions, the Wizard will provide you with information about relevant permits and licenses. Please do note that the Wizard is only meant as a starting point; requirements for your individual business may vary. 

 

The Wizard should take between 20 and 30 minutes to complete, and must be completed in one sitting. At the end, you can email the results to yourself, print them out, or save them as part of your profile. Please do not use your browser's back button while using the Wizard, as it may affect your results.
